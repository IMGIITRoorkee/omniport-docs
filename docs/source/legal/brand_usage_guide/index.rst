Brand usage guide
=================

This document guides on the use of the logos and brand imagery of both Omniport
and the Information Management Group.

All branding assets are available on GitHub. Feel free to use them when 
referring to Omniport or to IMG. Your use of these branding assets is subject
to a few basic rules.

Read on to understand what these rules are.
